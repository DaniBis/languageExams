### Which IELTS Are You Taking?

**Section Title:**  
Which IELTS Are You Taking?

**Academic IELTS**

Academic IELTS is required for university applications and academic programs. It tests your ability to understand and use English in an academic environment.

**What we'll focus on:**
- Reading academic texts and articles
- Writing Task 1: Describing graphs, charts, and diagrams
- Writing Task 2: Academic essays
- Academic vocabulary and formal language
- Speaking about academic and abstract topics

**General Training IELTS**

General Training IELTS is for immigration, work, or training programs. It tests your English in everyday and workplace situations.

**What we'll focus on:**
- Reading everyday texts and workplace materials
- Writing Task 1: Letters (formal and informal)
- Writing Task 2: Opinion essays
- Practical vocabulary for daily life
- Speaking about familiar topics and experiences

---

### What You'll Learn in IELTS Lessons

**Section Title:**  
What You'll Learn in IELTS Lessons

**Reading**

Master skimming and scanning techniques, learn time management for 40 questions, understand True/False/Not Given strategies, and practice matching and completion questions. All with real IELTS materials.

**Writing**

For Task 1, learn to describe graphs, charts, and processes (Academic) or write formal and informal letters (General). For Task 2, master essay structure, planning, and organization. Learn grammar and vocabulary for higher bands and avoid common mistakes.

**Listening**

Develop effective note-taking strategies, improve spelling and number accuracy, learn multiple choice strategies, and practice diagram labeling. We'll use official IELTS listening materials.

**Speaking**

Prepare for Part 1 (introduction questions), Part 2 (long turn/cue card), and Part 3 (discussion). Focus on fluency, pronunciation, coherence, and vocabulary to achieve higher band scores.

---

### How We'll Improve Your Band Score

**Section Title:**  
How We'll Improve Your Band Score

Understanding IELTS band scores is key to improvement. Here's how we'll help you reach your target:

**Step 1: Initial Assessment**

**Step 2: Target Weak Areas**  
Focus specifically on the skills that will increase your band score the most. No wasted time on what you already do well.

**Step 3: Practice with Official Format**  
Use real IELTS test materials and practice under exam conditions so you're fully prepared.

**Step 4: Track Improvement**  
Regular progress checks show you exactly how you're improving and what to focus on next.

**Common Goals:**
