# English with Daniel - Brand Identity Guide

## 1. Brand Name and Tagline

**Brand Name:** English with Daniel

**Tagline:** Clear English. Confident You.

---

## 2. Target Audience

### Primary Audience Segments

#### Romanian Students
- **Focus:** Cambridge B1/B2/C1 Preparation
- **Demographics:** Teens, university students, and adults
- **Goals:** Achieve Cambridge certification for academic or career advancement
- **Needs:** Structured exam preparation, grammar clarity, speaking confidence

#### Chinese Students
- **Focus:** IELTS, Cambridge, General English
- **Demographics:** University students and young professionals
- **Goals:** Study abroad, immigration, or international career opportunities
- **Needs:** IELTS band improvement, pronunciation training, exam strategies

#### General Characteristics
- **Age Range:** 13-45 years old
- **Education Level:** Secondary school to university and professional
- **Motivation:** Career advancement, academic requirements, personal growth
- **Common Challenges:** Speaking confidence, grammar complexity, exam anxiety

3. **Grammar Mastery**
   - Simplified explanations
   - Practical application
   - Common mistake correction

4. **Exam Techniques**
   - Strategy development
   - Time management
   - Question type familiarity
   - Practice test analysis

## 4. Tone of Voice

### Communication Guidelines

#### Warm and Welcoming
- Use friendly, encouraging language
- Make students feel valued and supported
- Show genuine interest in their progress

#### Calm and Reassuring
- Reduce anxiety about exams and mistakes
- Provide steady, consistent guidance
- Maintain a relaxed learning atmosphere

#### Modern and Accessible
- Use contemporary, relevant examples
- Avoid overly academic or formal language
- Keep explanations conversational

#### Simple and Clear
- Use straightforward English
- Avoid jargon when possible
- Explain complex terms when necessary

#### Supportive and Motivating
- Celebrate progress and achievements
- Provide constructive feedback
- Encourage continuous improvement

## 5. Core Services

### Cambridge Exam Preparation
- **B1 Preliminary (PET):** Foundation level exam preparation
- **B2 First (FCE):** Upper-intermediate certification training
- **C1 Advanced (CAE):** Advanced level mastery

**Includes:**
- All four skills: Reading, Writing, Listening, Speaking
- Exam format familiarization
- Practice tests and mock exams
- Time management strategies
- Feedback on practice materials

### IELTS Preparation
- **Academic IELTS:** For university applications
- **General Training IELTS:** For immigration and work

**Includes:**
- Band score improvement strategies
- Task-specific training (Task 1 & 2 Writing, Speaking Parts 1-3)
- Vocabulary building for academic contexts
- Pronunciation and fluency development

### General English
- **Speaking Practice:** Conversation skills and fluency
- **Grammar Fundamentals:** Clear, practical grammar instruction
- **Vocabulary Expansion:** Topic-based and functional vocabulary
- **Real-world Communication:** Practical English for daily situations

### Pronunciation Training
- **Accent Reduction:** Clear, neutral English pronunciation
- **Intonation and Rhythm:** Natural speech patterns
- **Problem Sound Practice:** Individual sound correction
- **Connected Speech:** Linking and natural flow

## 7. Visual Identity

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **White (#FFFFFF):** Main background color for clean, open spaces
- **Soft Blue (#6BA3D4 or #5B9BD5):** Primary brand color for trust and calm
- **Pastel Yellow (#FFE699 or #FFF4CC):** Accent color for warmth and optimism

#### Color Usage Guidelines
- **White:** Backgrounds, negative space, clean sections
- **Soft Blue:** Headings, buttons, key elements, links
- **Pastel Yellow:** Highlights, call-to-action accents, important notices

#### Color Psychology
- **White:** Clarity, simplicity, professionalism
- **Blue:** Trust, reliability, calm, expertise
- **Yellow:** Warmth, friendliness, optimism, encouragement

### Typography

#### Heading Font: Poppins
- **Style:** Modern, geometric, friendly
- **Usage:** Page titles, section headings, navigation
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)

#### Body Font: Inter
- **Style:** Clean, readable, professional
- **Usage:** Body text, paragraphs, descriptions, lists
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600)

#### Font Pairing Rationale
- Poppins provides friendly, approachable headings
- Inter ensures excellent readability for longer content
- Both fonts are modern and web-optimized
- Professional yet accessible aesthetic

### Design Principles

#### Clean and Modern
- Generous white space
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Simple, uncluttered layouts
- Focus on content over decoration

#### Professional Yet Friendly
- Balanced formality
- Approachable visuals
- Professional imagery
- Warm, inviting design elements

#### User-Friendly
- Intuitive navigation
- Clear calls-to-action
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading times

### Brand Voice Examples

#### What We Say
✅ "Let's make this grammar point crystal clear together."  
✅ "Your progress this week has been excellent!"  
✅ "Don't worry, this is a common mistake. Here's an easy way to fix it."  
✅ "You're ready for this exam. Let's practice your strategy."

#### What We Don't Say
❌ "This is very difficult. Most students struggle here."  
❌ "You should already know this."  
❌ "That's completely wrong."  
❌ "This grammar is too complex to explain simply."
