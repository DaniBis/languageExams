## Design & UX Guidelines Summary

### Color Usage
- **White (#FFFFFF):** Primary background, clean sections
- **Soft Blue (#6BA3D4):** CTAs, headings, links, active states
- **Pastel Yellow (#FFE699):** Highlights, badges, accent sections

### Typography
- **Headings:** Poppins (Semi-Bold or Bold)
- **Body:** Inter (Regular)
- **Font sizes:** Responsive, mobile-friendly

### CTAs (Call-to-Action Buttons)
- **Primary CTA:** Soft blue background, white text, rounded corners
- **Secondary CTA:** White background, soft blue border and text
- **Hover states:** Slightly darker blue
- **Mobile:** Full-width or prominent sizing

### Images & Icons
- Professional, friendly imagery
- Modern, simple icons
- Brand colors where possible
- Authentic photos (avoid overly stock-looking images)

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous white space
- Consistent padding and margins
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Mobile-first responsive design

### Readability
- Clear font sizes (minimum 16px body text)
- Good contrast ratios
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Bullet points and lists for scannability
- Avoid walls of text

### Conversion Optimization
- **Multiple CTAs:** Don't rely on just one CTA per page
- **Social proof:** Reviews and testimonials prominently featured
- **Urgency (subtle):** "Book your free consultation" not "Limited time only!"
- **Trust signals:** Clear policies, professional design, authentic testimonials
- **Reduce friction:** Simple forms, clear pricing, easy payment

### Mobile Responsiveness
- All pages must be fully responsive
